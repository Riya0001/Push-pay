Assembly and Setup

1. Take the device supplied and insert it into the I-offering clip.
2. Before you begin collecting, make sure the battery is charged.
3. To charge the battery, plug the USB-C Cable into the battery and the USB-A Cable into the wall plug.
4. Plug into a standard wall outlet for 3-4 hours for a full charge.
5. Remove the cable from the battery and clip the unit onto the offering basket to start collecting.
6. The battery run time is approximately 6-7 hours.
